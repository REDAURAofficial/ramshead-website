About the show

Adapted from the 1892 play about adolescents struggling into sexual awareness in a provincial German town, Spring Awakening speaks across centuries to the trials and triumphs of growing into adulthood. A winner of 8 Tony Awards, this play turned rock-musical is a foot-stamping, head-bobbing, poignant tribute to the resilience of adolescents.

Ram's Head is now hiring staff for the writing, production, and technical teams of Gaieties 2013! Applications for particular positions will be released beginning in January. If you are a Stanford student who is ready to bring fresh ideas, energy and humor to one of Stanford's oldest traditions, please feel free to email producer Nora Tjossem for more information about the various roles.

About the Show

Gaieties is an annual student written musical comedy aimed at uniting the Stanford campus. Performed the week before Big Game against Cal, the production is meant to remind students of the reasons why we love Stanford. For more informaiton, visit the show's website.

About the Show

The Original Winter One Acts are a beloved annual Stanford tradition dating back to the 1980s. Three one act plays, all written, directed, and performed by students, make their world premieres at Stanford and proudly showcase student talent in writing for the stage.
