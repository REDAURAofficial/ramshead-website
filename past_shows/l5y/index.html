Welcome to the Ram's Head Theatrical Society's 2012 Production of...

Jason Robert Brown's beloved musical

The Last Five Years

is a show about a relationship, about a marriage. It’s told from two angles. From the man’s point of view, it starts at the first meeting and ends in the present, when the marriage breaks up. From the woman’s point of view, it starts in the present and works back in time to the first meeting. Only at the wedding, in the center of the one-act evening, do the stories intersect. One has to do some thinking to track the two stories, but the clues are all there.

~ Richard Maltby, Jr.

Parking Update

We highly recommend biking or walking to the EPC if possible, as there is very limited parking. It is a lovely walk around Lake Lagunita and then you will end up right at the EPC. The closest available parking without a pass will be "C" parking, located in select areas of the Lagunita Parking lot (next to the EPC) and across the street by Roble Field. For questions about parking or handicap accessibility, please contact Randi Rudolph at rrudolph@stanford.edu.
