Definitions

Nominations for Eternal Ram and Service Awards are solicited from membership during Spring Quarter. The Board of Directors presents the awards at the Ram's Head Gala in May. More information can be found here.

Eternal Ram

Eternal Rams are meant to recognize those individuals who have consistently shown dedication to the aims and ideals of Ram's Head, devoted an exceptional amount of time to Ram's Head, and exhibited the drive to leave the organization better than they found it. Truly representing the professionalism and spirit of the organization, these individuals have been devoted to the organization over multiple productions and seasons, and they have left a positive legacy.

Service Award

Ram's Head Service Awards shall recognize Ram's Head members who have performed work above and beyond the call of duty in the service of the organization and its productions. In particular, they are designed to honor individuals who have consistently shown dedication to the aims and ideals of Ram's Head, but who may not necessarily have had the recognition associated with starring roles or major staff positions.
