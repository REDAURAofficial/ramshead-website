The Original Winter One Acts, also known as OWOA, is an annual performance of one act plays written, directed, and performed by Stanford students. This year, we are excited to present three exceptional one acts: Due Dates, Script Selector, and Occam's House.

Due Dates is a play written by Daniel Johnson '16 and directed by Nikke Hardson-Hurley '17.

Script Selector is a play written by Kiran Magar and directed by Adi Chang '18.

Occam's House is a play written by Brandon Silberstein and directed by Brandon Powell '14.

OWOA 2015 will be performing at Pigott Theater on January 15-17 at 8:00pm.
