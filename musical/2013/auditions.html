Auditions are open to all Stanford University students. No prior experience required; Ram's Head encourages people from all backgrounds to audition.

Auditioners must first sign up for a five-minute time slot by clicking on the "Click to Sign Up" button below. Please plan on arriving at least ten minutes early to sign in, and be sure to download and fill out our audition packet prior to showing up at auditions.

We ask that students prepare sixteen to thirty-two bars of vocal music to present a cappella, ideally in the genre of musical theater or contemporary rock. Students may also be asked to run scales in order to demonstrate vocal range. Pre-prepared monologues and dance-wear are not required; acting and dance auditions will take place at call-backs.


To sign up for an audition time-slot, please click on the link below. If you submit before January 7th, you should receive an email with a time-slot allocation by January 7th; if you submit post-January 7th, you should receive a response within 24 hours.
